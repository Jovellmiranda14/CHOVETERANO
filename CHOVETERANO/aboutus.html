About Us

When we started during the pandemic. We, cousins, decided to have a new haircut skill while in quarantine. With daily practice, we became better and more precise with our haircuts.Every month, we get stronger and stronger to build our own barbershop. After my cousins and I become extremely skilled hair stylists, my relatives bravely opened a barbershop where they continued cut hair.

We become part of our community, and we enjoy making our customers happy. Behind every haircut, you can see the hard work, determination, and unity that brought our little barbershop to life in the midst of the trials brought by the pandemic. Our story is an example of how collaboration in the midst of crisis can be inspiring. Thanks to our collaboration, determination, and hard work, our barbershop now has two branches.
